INFORMATION WE COLLECT

None. NFCommunicator does not collect any information about you or your
device.

HOW THE APP WORKS

NFCommunicator reads and writes encrypted text messages to physical NFC
tags. All processing happens locally on your Android device:

  - Messages you type are encrypted on-device before being written to an
    NFC tag.
  - Encryption uses AES-256-GCM with PBKDF2-HMAC-SHA256 key derivation.
  - A random salt and nonce are generated per message — no two encrypted
    payloads are the same.
  - The password you enter is used only during the current session and is
    never stored anywhere.
  - Decrypted message text exists only in memory for as long as you have
    it open on screen.

NETWORK ACCESS

The app declares no network permissions and makes no network connections of
any kind. There are no analytics SDKs, crash reporters, ad networks, or
third-party libraries that communicate over the internet.

NFC TAG DATA

Data written to an NFC tag is stored on the physical tag itself — not in
the cloud, not on our servers, and not in any account. Control of that data
belongs entirely to whoever possesses the physical tag.

PERMISSIONS

The app requests one permission:

  - NFC: required to read from and write to NFC tags. This permission is
    not used for any other purpose.

THIRD PARTIES

NFCommunicator does not share data with any third party because no data is
collected. The app contains no advertising, no in-app purchases, and no
third-party SDKs that access user data.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The app does not collect personal information from anyone, including
children under the age of 13. It is rated Everyone on Google Play.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

If the privacy practices of NFCommunicator ever change, an updated policy
will be published at this URL with a revised effective date. Given that the
app collects no data, we do not anticipate material changes.
