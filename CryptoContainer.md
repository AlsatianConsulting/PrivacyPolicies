# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

CryptoContainer ("the app") is published by Alsatian Consulting. This policy explains what data the app handles and how it is used.

## Summary

- No account is required.
- No analytics or advertising SDKs are used.
- No personal data is sold or shared.
- Core encryption and container operations run locally on your device.

## Information We Collect

CryptoContainer does not collect personal information from you for analytics, advertising, profiling, or remote account management.

The app does not operate a cloud backend for normal app use.

## Information Processed On Device

To perform requested actions, the app processes data you explicitly provide or select, such as:

- files and folders you choose
- encrypted containers (`.hc`) and AESCrypt files (`.aes`)
- passwords, keyfiles, and PIM values you enter

This processing is done locally on your device to encrypt, decrypt, mount, open, import, export, and share files.

## Temporary Storage and Retention

Some operations may create temporary files in app-private storage (for example, handling shared files, staging outputs, or preparing imports/exports).

Temporary files are intended to be removed after the related operation completes or when the app session ends.

Session secrets (such as entered passwords) are kept only as needed for active operations and are not intended for long-term storage.

## Sharing and Third-Party Apps

CryptoContainer only shares files with other apps when you explicitly trigger Android share/open actions.

After a file is shared to another app, that app’s privacy and security practices apply to its handling of the file.

## Network Use

Core features do not require an internet connection. The app is designed for local, on-device workflows.

## Permissions

CryptoContainer requests only permissions needed for its feature set (for example, file access via Android document pickers, foreground service behavior during long-running operations, and notifications for user-visible operation status).

## Third-Party Components

The app bundles open-source cryptographic and filesystem components (including VeraCrypt-related, NTFS, exFAT, and FAT support libraries) to provide local functionality.

These components are included within the app package and are not remote data collectors.

## Children's Privacy

CryptoContainer is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted in this document with a revised "Last updated" date.
